Routes to Explore

  • Abruzzi Spur

    The route with climbers tackling this pass located on Pakistan side. Route name from Duke of the Abruzzi

  • North Ridge

    Most dangerous route to the summit. Two teams can dare the attempt at a time. This route is at the Chinese side

  • Magic Line

    Considered to be the second most demanding. It was first climbed in 1986 by a Polish-Slovak expedition

  • Polish Line

    First climbed in 1986, the climbers were killed on the descent. Entrance to this route is via the Pakistan side

  • West Face

    First ascended in 2007 by Russian team. It is Entirely comprised of rock crevasses and snow capped couloirs
